## Why the first scan is free

A new document tool must prove that it finds useful problems before it asks for payment. The beta therefore emphasizes adoption, repeat usage, and accuracy testing.

## What should remain free

The local, privacy-first document hygiene scan should remain available without an account. Paid plans should fund services that create additional cost or responsibility, such as external bibliographic database queries, persistent project history, batch processing, and human verification.

## No acceptance guarantees

No plan can guarantee peer-review success, editorial acceptance, or perfect compliance with every journal's instructions. The product is a quality-control layer, not an official journal certification.
